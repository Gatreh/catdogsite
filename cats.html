Why not have a cat-nap?

Powernapping for champions!

Conservation of energy

Cat's nap a lot to conserve energy for when they need to pounce on their prey. That means they'll always be ready and alert during their waking hours.

For that, We need to learn how to cat-nap like a true feline to make the most of our day.

Napping intervals

Naps should be taken often. If you aren't fighting, stalking or eating you should be napping in order to prepare for these. Beware however that you might get attacked while you nap!
